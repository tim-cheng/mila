Parent2D

Building the largest parent network on mobile

Connect with your fellow parents and get what you need as a parent quickly

Ask Questions. Share Insight. Get Info. On the Go.

As busy parents, we don't have time to search online archaic forum posts or join huge social network groups where you don't even know who you are talking to. Introducing Parent2D, the simpliest way to stay connected with parents you trust and help each other be better parents on the go.

Get parenting questions answered with your private parents social network

Parent2D lets you interact with your parents connections and their connections, so you get information from your trusted resources. Parents are in turn more willing to help each other out.

Sharing event info or product insights have never been so easy.

No time to plan for play dates? No problem. Just post and play by ear. Love some baby products? Share with your parents connections so they can save some time. By helping parents out with useful and relevant information, we can support each other on the toughest job in the world!

Need a mental break or just a place to decompress after a tough parenting episode?

Use some creativity and write a quick thought, or just get comfort feeling for other parents struggles. Sometimes empathy is all we need. Parent2D can be your therapy.

Easy grouping of parents

Parents in the same area, parents having similar age kids, parents sharing same cultural background, your interaction can always be relevant and efficient.

Secured 2 degree private network

2-way connection by confirmation, visibility to your connections network bring you both safety and expanded resources

State of the art design

Say good-bye to archaic online parenting forums, and say hello to modern, simplistic and beautiful design that is long overdue for the 400M parents online.
